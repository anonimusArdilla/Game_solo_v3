# Economy

## Currency

- Coins: earned from gameplay, spent on cosmetics and themes.

## Sources

- Level completion rewards (scaled by difficulty)
- Performance bonuses (no-hit streaks, perfect gates)
- Daily login reward (streak-based)
- Rewarded ads (optional)

## Sinks

- Skins (one-time purchase)
- Trails (one-time purchase)
- Theme palettes (one-time purchase)
- Optional continue tokens purchased with coins to reduce ad reliance

## Anti-Inflation Controls

- Daily rewarded-ad coin cap (remote-config controlled)
- Increasing cosmetic price curve per world tier
